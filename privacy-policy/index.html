Privacy Policy

How Dicno Labs approaches privacy across its website, apps, tools, and learning resources.

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Overview

Dicno Labs builds practical software and content with a privacy-conscious approach. This policy explains what information may be collected when you use our website, apps, tools, or related resources.

Information we may collect

We may collect basic usage information such as pages visited, device or browser type, approximate region, and technical diagnostics. If you contact Dicno Labs, we may receive the information you choose to send.

Apps and advertising

Dicno Labs apps may use platform services, analytics, or advertising services such as Google Play, Google AdMob, or Google AdSense where applicable. These services may process data according to their own policies and settings.

How information is used

Information is used to operate the website, improve apps and content, respond to messages, maintain security, and support advertising or monetization where enabled.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the contact page or by email at support@dicnolabs.com.
