Refinance Calculator FAQ

What is refinancing?

Refinancing replaces an existing mortgage with a new loan.

What is a break-even point?

It is the number of months needed for estimated savings to recover closing costs.

Can refinancing increase total interest?

Yes. Extending the term can lower payments but may increase total interest over time.

Does this include taxes and insurance?

No. It compares principal and interest payments only.

What if monthly savings are negative?

That means the new estimated payment is higher than the current estimated payment.

Are closing costs always paid upfront?

Not always. Some loans roll costs into the balance, which changes the true comparison.
