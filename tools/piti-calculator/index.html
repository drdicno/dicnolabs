PITI Calculator FAQ

What does PITI stand for?

PITI stands for principal, interest, taxes, and insurance.

Does PITI include PMI?

PMI is not part of the acronym, but this calculator includes optional monthly PMI for a more complete estimate.

Why split principal and interest?

Splitting them shows how much of the payment reduces the loan balance versus how much pays borrowing cost.

Are taxes and insurance exact?

No. They are estimates and can change by location, property, insurer, and year.

Does this include HOA dues?

No. Use the Mortgage Calculator if you want to include HOA dues in a broader payment estimate.

Is this a loan approval tool?

No. It is an educational planning calculator, not a lender approval or quote.
