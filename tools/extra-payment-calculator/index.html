Extra Payment Calculator FAQ

How do extra payments save interest?

Extra payments reduce principal sooner, leaving less balance for future interest to accrue on.

Do extra payments always shorten the loan?

They usually do when applied to principal, but loan servicer rules can vary.

Should extra payments go to principal?

For payoff acceleration, they typically need to be applied to principal.

Does this include taxes or insurance?

No. This tool focuses on loan principal and interest only.

Can I compare with refinancing?

Yes. Use the Refinance Calculator to compare rate changes and break-even timing.

Is this a guaranteed payoff schedule?

No. It is an educational estimate based on fixed monthly assumptions.
