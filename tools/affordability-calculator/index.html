Home Affordability FAQ

What is home affordability?

Home affordability is an estimate of the home price or payment that may fit a buyer's income, debts, and cash available.

What debt-to-income ratio does this use?

This calculator uses a 36% total DTI planning target by default.

Is this a mortgage approval?

No. Lenders use their own underwriting rules, credit review, documentation, and product requirements.

Why does down payment matter?

A larger down payment can reduce the loan amount and may improve monthly payment estimates.

Do taxes and insurance affect affordability?

Yes. Higher taxes or insurance reduce the payment available for principal and interest.

Can rates change affordability?

Yes. Higher interest rates usually lower the affordable home price for the same payment budget.
