Amortization Calculator FAQ

What is amortization?

Amortization is the process of paying down a loan through scheduled payments over time.

Why does interest start higher?

Interest is based on the remaining balance, which is largest early in the loan.

Does the table show every month?

No. This page shows a basic yearly summary to keep the schedule easy to scan.

Does this include taxes or insurance?

No. It focuses on principal and interest only.

Can extra payments change amortization?

Yes. Extra principal payments can reduce interest and shorten payoff time.

Is this an official lender schedule?

No. It is an educational estimate.
